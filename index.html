About FroCat Studios

We are a small Roblox studio with big ambitions and a very human approach.

We started simple

We are building from the ground up, and every part of the studio matters. No fake scale, no empty promises, just real work and real people.

We value people over polish

The best ideas usually come from people who care. We want creators who are thoughtful, dependable, and excited to make things that actually feel good.

We grow together

Our Discord is where the studio feels alive. It is where we talk, share, test ideas, and welcome the next people who want to help build it.

Staff opportunities
